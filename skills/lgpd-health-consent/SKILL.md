# LGPD Health Consent

Use explicit user consent before triage processing.

## Required consent boxes

1. Health data processing for triage summary.
2. Voice capture and transcription consent.
3. Privacy policy acceptance with data retention period.

## Data retention

- Keep retained records for up to 30 days.
- Prune expired records automatically on app load and on write.
- Allow easy data reset in UI where possible.

## Safety wording

- Keep medical disclaimer permanently visible.
- State the assistant is educational and does not replace medical consultation.
